About Us

PARI is an organisation established to meet the needs of the souls unheard during the hardest of times. PARI is aimed at creating an organized distribution of relief materials during the disaster. We try our best to bridge the gap between the people and the NGOs. PARI makes sure that the donations are in safe hands and are reached safely.
