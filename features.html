Donations can be made as per your comfort. You can donate money or you can give us the referrence code of the order of relief materials you've placed online or you can buy products from our page. All the donations will be directed towars NGOs.

SMS Feature

We understand that most of the times during disasters, we won't have access to internet. So we have an SMS feature. You can send SMS to us with the relief materials required and the location. Our NLP algorithm will take care of it!
